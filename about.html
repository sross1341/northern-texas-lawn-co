About Northern Texas Lawn Co.

At Northern Texas Lawn Co., we believe homeowners should be able to enjoy their yards without giving up precious free time. We launched our business in March 2026 with a clear promise: to help homeowners “enjoy their yard without the yard work.” Our announcement highlighted core services—lawn mowing and edging, weekly or bi‑weekly service, seasonal cleanups and landscaping—and invited North Texas friends to let us take the yard work off their weekend list.

Northern Texas Lawn Co. is locally owned and operated. We’re rooted in the corridor north of Dallas and serve Van Alstyne, Anna, Celina and surrounding towns. These communities are experiencing rapid growth while maintaining small‑town charm. Real‑estate analysts note that Van Alstyne, Celina and Anna have become attractive destinations for home buyers seeking a blend of affordable living and expanding amenities. Anna, in particular, sits about 40 miles north of downtown Dallas and has seen steady growth over the past decade while offering a quieter pace of life.

Our mission is to provide dependable, high‑quality lawn care that frees up your time and keeps our neighborhood landscapes healthy. We incorporate proven horticultural practices to promote thick, resilient turf and thriving plants. Whether you need routine mowing, seasonal cleanup or help revitalizing your landscape, we approach every yard with care, professionalism and respect for the environment.

Thank you for considering Northern Texas Lawn Co. We look forward to partnering with you to make your yard a place to relax and enjoy.
